Our Story

Built for businesses ready to modernize

At Orbitlance, we believe technology should do more than just solve problems—it should help businesses grow. Our mission is to build intelligent software, AI assistants, and automation solutions that simplify operations, improve efficiency, and create better experiences for both businesses and their customers.

Whether you need an AI assistant, workflow automation, a custom CRM, or a fully tailored web application, we design every solution around the way your business works.

Our solutions can help you:

🤖 Answer customer questions instantly with AI assistants.
📦 Track orders and provide real-time updates.
🏨 Manage hotel reservations and payment confirmations.
🍽️ Process recurring food or service orders.
⚙️ Automate repetitive workflows and internal operations.
📊 Build custom CRM systems to manage customers, sales, and business data.
💻 Develop custom software that grows with your business.

Unlike traditional software, our AI solutions continue to evolve. As your business changes, we help improve and expand your systems so they remain aligned with your goals.

And because our AI assistants are available 24/7, your customers can receive fast, reliable support anytime—day or night.

At Orbitlance, we don't just build software. We build intelligent digital solutions that help businesses work smarter, scale faster, and stay ahead.
